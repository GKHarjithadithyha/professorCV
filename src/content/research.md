My primary research interests lie at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and real-world system applications.

### Key Focus Areas

*   **Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI):** Developing transparent AI models for critical decision-making systems.
*   **Machine Learning and Deep Learning:** Advanced architectures for predictive analytics and pattern recognition.
*   **Intelligent Transportation Systems:** AI-driven solutions for traffic management, safety, and autonomous vehicle networks (VANETs).
*   **IoT & Cloud Computing:** Secure and efficient data sharing architectures using Wireless Sensor Networks.
*   **Computer Vision:** Object detection (YOLO variants) for smart city applications and bio-waste management.
*   **AI for Sustainability:** Leveraging technology for smart cities, agriculture (crop disease detection), and environmental monitoring.
