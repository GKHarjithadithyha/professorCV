## 🧠 Short Bio

Dr. N. Bharathiraja is an Associate Professor and active researcher in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Cloud-enabled IoT systems. He completed his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Anna University and is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Kuala Lumpur University of Science & Technology, Malaysia. His research focuses on intelligent transportation systems, explainable AI, IoT-based data sharing, and computer vision applications for real-world problems. With over 15 years of teaching experience, he has published extensively in SCIE and Scopus-indexed journals, holds multiple patents, and actively supervises student research.
